About Cipher BSK Password Manager

Secure, open, and user-controlled password encryption.

Developed by: Sazzad Saju, Software Developer

Encryption Model: Based on Cipher-BSK, a published symmetric encryption model. Users can choose encryption strength by selecting the number of rounds — typically, one round is sufficient.

End-to-End Encryption

Your data is encrypted locally using your master password. No one else, not even we, can decrypt it.

Privacy First

We never track or store your master password or vault contents. You're in full control.

Cross-Device Sync

Securely access your encrypted data from any device with Chrome Extension login.

Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Here's how we protect it.

No Tracking: We don’t collect personal data or use analytics.

Local Encryption: All data is encrypted on your device using your master password. We never see or store it.

Encrypted Data Only: When you save, we receive only encrypted content — never plaintext.

Encryption Control: You choose the number of encryption rounds. One round is usually enough, but more can add extra protection.

Security Enforcement: Saving is blocked if encryption rounds are set to zero (plaintext). We enforce encryption to protect your data.

User-Controlled Output: You control how your data is encrypted and how it looks after encryption.

Open Source: Our code is public and auditable for transparency.
